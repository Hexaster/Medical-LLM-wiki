# Therapeutics

Therapeutics covers treatment, supportive care, prevention of complications, surveillance, rehabilitation, procedures, medications, and other interventions used to manage diseases or health conditions. In this wiki, therapeutic summaries are for research and study context only and are not treatment recommendations.

## Role in the wiki

- Disease and syndrome pages may link here when summarizing management and surveillance sections from educational or guideline sources.
- Therapeutic claims should distinguish established guideline recommendations, expert consensus, clinical trial evidence, investigational therapy, and supportive care.
- Safety, contraindications, patient-specific factors, and regulatory status should be handled cautiously and attributed to sources.
