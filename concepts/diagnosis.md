# Diagnosis

Diagnosis is the clinical process of identifying a disease, syndrome, injury, or health condition from history, examination, testing, imaging, pathology, genetic findings, and other evidence. In this wiki, diagnosis pages and disease pages should distinguish suggestive clinical findings, confirmatory tests, differential diagnosis, and the limits of available evidence.

## Role in the wiki

- Disease and syndrome pages may link here when summarizing diagnostic criteria, testing approaches, and differential diagnosis.
- Genetic and rare-disease pages may include diagnostic approaches such as chromosomal microarray, sequencing, targeted testing, biochemical testing, imaging, or clinical criteria.
- Diagnostic statements should be attributed to sources and should not be treated as medical advice.
