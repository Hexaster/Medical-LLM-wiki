# Pathology

Pathology is the study and characterization of disease processes, including mechanisms, structural or functional abnormalities, clinical manifestations, laboratory findings, and relationships between genotype, phenotype, environment, and disease expression.

## Role in the wiki

- Disease, syndrome, and genetic-condition pages may link here when summarizing clinical characteristics, mechanisms, and manifestations.
- Pathology summaries should distinguish observed features, proposed mechanisms, epidemiologic associations, and unresolved uncertainty.
- For genetic disorders, pathology may include molecular mechanism, inheritance pattern, penetrance, expressivity, and genotype-phenotype relationships.
