No therapy exists to prevent the multisystem and sometimes progressive organ involvement of BBS. Individuals with BBS require coordinated multidisciplinary care to formulate and coordinate management and therapeutic interventions.

## Genetic counseling

Bardet-Biedl syndrome (BBS) is typically inherited in an autosomal recessive manner. Note: (1) Although oligogenic inheritance of BBS has been suggested in some families with individuals who are identified as having clinical features of BBS and variants in two or more different BBS-associated genes , this mode of inheritance has been refuted in other studies . Identification of copy number variants and intronic variants and more precise phenotypic characterization of individuals with variants of uncertain significance are likely to provide more evidence for traditional autosomal recessive inheritance in BBS . (2) The clinical relevance of modifier gene variants in BBS is controversial at this time .
