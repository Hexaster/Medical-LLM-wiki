## Overview

FASTKD2 -related combined oxidative phosphorylation deficiency ( FASTKD2 -COXPD) is a multisystem disorder that can present from infancy to adulthood with developmental delay with regression that is often triggered by febrile illness and/or seizures. Additional neurologic findings include movement disorder, episodes of acute encephalomyopathy, abnormal muscle tone, and/or stroke-like episodes. Reported ocular manifestations include optic atrophy, nystagmus, strabismus, and visual impairment.
