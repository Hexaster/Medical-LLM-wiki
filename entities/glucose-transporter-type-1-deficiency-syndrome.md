## Overview

Glucose transporter type 1 deficiency syndrome (Glut1DS) is a disorder of brain energy metabolism. Glucose, the essential metabolic fuel for the brain, is transported into the brain exclusively by the protein glucose transporter type 1 (Glut1) across the endothelial cells forming the blood-brain barrier (BBB). Glut1DS results from the inability of Glut1 to transfer sufficient glucose across the BBB to meet the glucose demands of the brain.
