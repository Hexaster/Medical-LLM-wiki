## Overview

ALPK1 -related autoinflammatory disease ( ALPK1 -AD) is characterized by clinical findings that can include intraocular inflammation, retinal degeneration, recurrent fever, deforming arthritis, and headaches. Anhidrosis/hypohidrosis, dental caries, short dental roots, and hyposalivation are common. While most adults have ophthalmologic manifestations, vision loss is not universal.

## Clinical characteristics

ALPK1 -related autoinflammatory disease ( ALPK1 -AD) is characterized by clinical findings that can include intraocular inflammation, retinal degeneration, recurrent fever, deforming arthritis, and headaches. Anhidrosis/hypohidrosis with associated findings of enamel and caries, short dental roots, and hyposalivation are common. Most adults have ophthalmologic manifestations; however, vision loss is not universal .
