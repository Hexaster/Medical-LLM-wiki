## Overview

The spectrum of ANO5- related muscle disease is a continuum that ranges from asymptomatic hyperCKemia and exercise-induced myalgia to proximal and/or distal muscle weakness. The most typical presentation is limb-girdle muscular dystrophy R12 anoctamin-5-related (LGMD-R12) with late-onset proximal lower limb weakness in the fourth or fifth decade (range: age 15-70 years). Less common is Miyoshi muscular dystrophy 3 (MMD3) with adult-onset calf distal myopathy (onset around age 30 years).

## Clinical characteristics
