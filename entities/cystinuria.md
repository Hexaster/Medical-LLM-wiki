## Overview

Cystinuria is characterized by early-onset and recurrent kidney stones composed of cystine. Individuals with cystinuria often present with their first kidney stone in the first two decades of life. Complications of cystinuria include recurrent urinary tract infections, chronic kidney disease (CKD), and hypertension.

## Clinical characteristics

Cystinuria is characterized by early-onset and recurrent kidney stones composed of cystine. Complications include recurrent urinary tract infections, chronic kidney disease (CKD), and hypertension. Some individuals develop end-stage kidney disease (ESKD).
