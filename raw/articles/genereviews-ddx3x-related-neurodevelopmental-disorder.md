## Summary
DDX3X -related neurodevelopmental disorder ( DDX3X -NDD) typically occurs in females and very rarely in males. All affected individuals reported to date have developmental delay / intellectual disability ranging from mild to severe; about 50% of affected girls remain nonverbal after age five years. Hypotonia, a common finding, can be associated with feeding difficulty in infancy.

## Diagnosis
Formal diagnostic criteria for DDX3X- related neurodevelopmental disorder ( DDX3X -NDD) have not been established. DDX3X -NDD can be considered in an individual with several of the following clinical and brain imaging findings .
